"STAY SAFE ONLINE" CHECKLIST FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS

1.ENABLE TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION(2FA): ALWAYS USE 2FA FOR YOUR UNIVERSITY EMAIL,GITHUB,AND SOCIAL MEDIA ACCOUNTS TO PREVENT UNAUTHORIZED LOGINS.

2.THE"PIN is for Paying"RULE: REMEMBER THAT YOU NEVER NEED TO ENTER YOUR UPI PIN OR SCAN A QR CODE TO RECIEVE ONEY.IF SOMEONE ASKS YOU TO ENTER A PIN TO VERIFY A REFUND OR PRIZE,IT IS A SCAM.

3.VERIFY UPI GLOBAL/MERCHANT IDs: BEFORE CLICKING PAY ON APPS LIKE GPAY,PAYTM DOUBLE CHECK THE VERIFIED NAME OF THE RECIPIENT.

4.USE"FIND MY DEVICE"& SIM LOCK: ENSURE YOUR SMARTPHONE HAS A SIM CARD PIN AND "FIND MY DEVICE" ENABLED.

5.AVOID PUBLIC WI-FI FOR SENSITIVE TASKS: NEVER LOG INTO YOUR STUDENT PORTAL,BANK OR LINKEDLN WHILE CONNECTED TO FREE PUBLIC WI-FI.USE A VPN INSTEAD

6.AUDIT APP PERMISSIONS: REGULARLY CHECK WHICH APPS HAVE ACCESS TO YOUR CONTACTS,MICROPHONE,AND SMS.

7.IDENTIFY PHISHING ON WHATSAPP: BE AWARE OF MESSAGES OFFERING"PART TIME JOB OFFERS" OR "STOCK MARKET TIPS".IF YOU DIDNT SIGN UP FOR IT,ITS LIKELY A SCAM DESIGNED TO STEAL YOUR DATA.

SECURE YOUR AADHAR: USE mAADHAAR APP TOLOCK YOUR BIOMETRICS.THIS PREVENTS ANYONE FROM USING YOUR FINGERPRINT FOR UNAUTHORIZED TRANSACTIONS OR SIM CARD INSURANCES IN YOUR NAME.
